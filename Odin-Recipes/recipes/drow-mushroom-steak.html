Drow Mushroom Steak

Description

A delicacy of the great Underdark City of Spider, Menzoberranzan, these seasoned ripplebark mushroom steaks are harvested fresh from the fungi fields near Lake Donigarten. While their exact agronomy is a closely guarded drow secret, many speculate that fertilizer from the Donigarten island rothe make these mushrooms grow unusually large, meaty, and nutrient rich.

The dish has become so popular that it has migrated to the surface, and it's even rumored to be a favorite of Xanathar of Waterdeep. But make sure to carefully read the label on the bottle of balsamic vinegar, the drow are as famous for their poisons as their sauces.
